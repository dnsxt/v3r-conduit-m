Delete Windows wheels:
cd C:\users\source-1\desktop\rag-deploy\wheels

Download Linux-compatible wheels (single command):

  torch torchvision ^
  sentence-transformers==2.3.0 ^

Verify:

Confirm filenames contain cp312 + linux_x86_64 or manylinux.
Rebuild:

  --platform linux/amd64 ^
  --provenance false ^
  --sbom false ^

  -t 236510207245.dkr.ecr.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/rag-ingest-container:ml-offline-final ^

Deploy & Test: Tag ml-offline-final, Memory 2048 MB, Timeout 10 min, test with {}.
